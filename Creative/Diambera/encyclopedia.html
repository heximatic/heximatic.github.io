    Word beginnings for numbers, base 10 system. U-Celestial. To make strings of numbers, word is appended before the other number; for example,

    255 hours is Dia-Fen-Fenmor.

    35 days is Cit-Fenmi.

    However, when writing dates, it is flipped the other way-

    Day 12 is written as Diam-Aomi, or (2-1). Day 21, then, is Ali-Diami (1-2).

    Additionally, si, when adding more 0s after it, proceeds with 'zo'. 

    100 = Ali-Sizo, 1000= Ali-Sizozo.

    NOVAs

    Neutron Originating Variable Aliens. These beings are small parts split off from the stars and their power levels and abilities are based on what sort of star they came from, but is always above Mortal. 

    NOVNs

    Neutron Originating Variable Natives. These beings are artificially and unethically created by splitting apart the atoms in a being’s body, and then inserting a CORE at the center of their mass before their matter drifts off. These beings will be akin to lower-mass stars in the amount of energy that they have, and sometimes will only be visible at the infrared or lower. However, they are able to make their matter travel long distances and see off in places that most people are unable to get to. Their cores tend to be quite fragile though, so any major damage means certain death. Additionally, the process of becoming a NOVN is known to be very painful, hence the ‘unethical’ part.
