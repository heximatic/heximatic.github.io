Explanation

Any time there is a complaint about something fairly trivial (something that can easily be solved or has little to no effect on general experience), a tally is added. However, if a compliment or a nice remark is made, a tally is removed.

Indeed, I do recognize the hypocrisy in making a complaint in something relatively trivial, but, as my mother has put it, the build-up of those sorts of feelings generally leads to a negative or stressful 'vibe'. This is primarily a positive-punishment (adding to the experience negatively) exersize.

Additionally, as to be fair, I have also included my own buttons.
